FAQ Section

This page contains frequently asked questions about our trucking services.

We offer full truckload (FTL), less-than-truckload (LTL), intermodal transportation, dedicated fleet services, and last-mile delivery solutions. Our network covers all 48 contiguous states with over 500 terminals nationwide. We specialize in temperature- controlled shipments, hazmat transportation, and oversized freight handling.
You can track your shipment in real-time using our online portal or mobile app. Simply enter your PRO number or bill of lading number to get instant updates on location, estimated delivery time, and any exceptions. Our AI-powered visibility platform provides predictive ETAs with 95% accuracy.
We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint through fleet modernization, alternative fuels, and route optimization. Our initiatives include transitioning to electric vehicles for last-mile delivery, using biodiesel blends for long-haul, implementing idle-reduction technology, and investing in carbon offset programs. We have achieved a 25% reduction in emissions per mile since 2020.

Additional Information

Driver requirements and qualifications

All drivers must have a valid CDL-A license, minimum 2 years of experience, clean MVR, and pass DOT physical and drug screening. We provide ongoing training in safety, fuel efficiency, and customer service. Benefits include competitive pay, health insurance, 401k matching, and paid time off.

Insurance and liability coverage

We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including $1M auto liability, $100K cargo insurance, and $5M umbrella policy. All shipments are protected against loss or damage. Additional coverage options are available for high-value or specialized freight.
